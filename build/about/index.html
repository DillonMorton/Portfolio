About Us

When it comes down to it, we are interested in providing an ever-rotating collection of clothes & products that push our desire for expression through the means of a wearable medium. By making each release limited to a small one-time run, we are able to focus on what we truly care about: continually creating new products that strive for exploration, creativity, and quality.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your exchange policy?

In order to provide our customers with the latest in stock, we must insist that all sales are final due to sizes being so limited for each design.

What shipping carrier do you use?

All orders will be shipped via USPS from Denver, Colorado.

How do I keep my new clothes fitting so well?

In order to keep our clothes fitting the same as the day you received them we recommend air drying them after washing in cold water.

Do you ship internationally?

We only ship orders nation wide at the moment, but we are working to find a solution to ship internationally.
