True Lust
View Project
True Lust (previously known as LUST), of Denver, Colorado, is the disco-noir synth outfit of Dillon Morton with Robert Sands. Creating a sound that whispers a slow, yet frantic sense of longing alongside an untold narrative of dark streets and hidden strangers, it walks a drunken line between longing and fear.
